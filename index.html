This website is about Julio Cortazar

Julio Florencio Cortazar, Argentinian, was one of the most influential Latin-American writers of the 20th century.

Some of his work.
"Rayuela" is Cortazar's most famous and important novel, published in 1963.
"Bestiario" it's his first short-stories book, published in 1951 in South America.
His second short-stories book, "Final del Juego", is one of his most influential works.
His fourth novel, "El Libro de Manuel", is a very political and controversial.
